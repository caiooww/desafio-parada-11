About Us

Our story began in 2010, when we opened our first restaurant in the heart of New York City. We were a small family business, but we had a big dream: to make the best pizza in the world. And we did it! Our pizza became famous all over the city, and soon people from all over the world were coming to taste it. Today, we have restaurants in every corner of the globe, but we still make our pizza the same way we did back then: with love, passion, and the best ingredients. Come and taste the magic!

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to make people happy. We believe that good food has the power to bring people together, to create memories, and to make the world a better place. That's why we put our heart and soul into every pizza we make, and why we always greet our customers with a smile. So come and join us on this delicious journey, and let us show you the magic of Fried Pizza!
