What is Fried Pizza

Fried Pizza is a family-owned restaurant that has been serving delicious pizza since 2010. Our pizza is famous for its thin crust, fresh ingredients, and unique flavors. We have restaurants all over the world, but our heart is still in New York City, where it all began. So come and join us on this delicious journey!

Our Menu

Our menu is a journey through the flavors of Italy. From the classic Margherita to the exotic Hawaiian, we have a pizza for every taste. And if you're not in the mood for pizza, don't worry! We also have a wide selection of pasta, salads, and desserts. And of course, everything is made with the same love and passion that made our pizza famous. So come and join us on this delicious adventure!
